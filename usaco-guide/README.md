## Stuff from the video

I got a good feeling when watching this specific video, seems like a good way of learning, some of the recommendations are:
- Always select problems that are difficult enough for you
- Immerse yourself into the community (watching streams and videos of people solving these problems)
- Allocate the time needed to evolve

- Give yourself ~30 minutes (I feel that is a lot) of being stuck before reading editorial
- If the editorial is needed, read it bit-by-bit and process it
- Note down what you're doing/thinking
